Flan

A tasty and family-favorite flan. Easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can of evaporated milk
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Making the flan:
    1. Melt sugar in a saucepan over medium-low heat until it is liquefied and has a gold color.
    2. Pour the melted sugar into a deep round glass baking dish and set it aside.
    3. Add the three eggs into a large bowl. Then add the condensed milk, evaporated milk, and the vanilla. Mix everything until it is smooth and pour the mixture into the dish that has the melted sugar.
    4. Bake in the preheated oven for about 1 hour. Once done, let the flan cool to room temperature. Cover the flan with a plastic wrap and put it in the fridge for 3 hours.
    5. After the 3 hours the flan is ready to be served and enjoyed.
